Django Girls KC Mentor Director, 2019-present

Django Girls is a yearly event facilitated by Kansas City Women in Technology. My responsibilities as Mentor Director have included recruitment of mentors; planning and organization of the event; placement of mentors and attendees in small groups; and design of custom swag.

Crit Night, a project in Django

Crit Night is designed to accompany an informal art discussion event held at a Kansas City venue. This Django application incorporates: Moderator and Artist accounts, Logins with validation, participants may create, read, update and delete their own artwork entries, and a star-rating voting system with comment fields for contributing to the artistic dialog. Please create an account, and be sure to use small images, preferably .png format.
